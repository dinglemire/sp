3. üìù Notes

  • Fix Compression (7): Going higher (15+) creates plastic skin. Keep at 7.
  • Recover Detail (5): Going higher (20+) brings back pixelated blocks from the web source.
  • Grain: Essential to stop AV1 from smoothing out skin texture at 32Mb/s.
  • MKV Container: Required for best AV1 compatibility on PC.
  • Dehalo:The Issue: Studio lighting (common in Brazzers) creates harsh contrast between a brightly lit actor and a dark background.
